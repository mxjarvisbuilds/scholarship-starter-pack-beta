How it works

Designed around the moment a family decides to start.

01

Begin with the student’s story

The intake asks for the context that often matters: location, grade, interests, responsibilities, service, work, and possible career direction.

02

Surface a thoughtful Best Match

The beta prepares one strongest starting point with rationale, document needs, essay direction, and official-source reminders.

03

Keep the rest organized

Families can review visible beta matches, update statuses, check off tasks, and print the plan for review.

04

Stay honest about uncertainty

The product helps with preparation. It does not promise awards, eligibility, deadlines, admissions, or financial aid outcomes.

Free guided intake

No account is required. Your answers stay in this browser during the beta.

Include anything that shows commitment: volunteer work, church leadership, sports, clubs, student leadership, family responsibilities, paid work, or community involvement.

College, trade school, skilled trades, vocational programs, and apprenticeships all count.

This tool is for high school students and families. Students under 18 are welcome to use it; reviewing with a parent or guardian is encouraged but not required.
